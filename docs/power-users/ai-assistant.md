# AI assistant

Mindwtr includes optional AI features for people who want help clarifying tasks, summarizing notes, or working with their system through a language interface.

## When to use this

Use AI features when they save effort after capture. Do not use them as a replacement for deciding what your commitments mean.

Good uses:

- Turn a vague inbox item into a clearer next action
- Summarize a long note
- Suggest a project outline
- Ask questions about your current task data

## Privacy model

AI is opt-in. Core capture, task editing, GTD workflow, and sync do not require AI.

::: warning Provider choice matters
If you connect a remote AI provider, the content you send is handled by that provider. Use a local model or avoid AI features when task content is sensitive.
:::

## Setup checklist

1. Choose a supported AI provider or local model path.
2. Add credentials through the app settings or local environment supported by your platform.
3. Test with non-sensitive sample data.
4. Keep AI disabled for workflows where you do not want data sent outside the device.

## Keep capture simple

Mindwtr intentionally keeps Inbox capture simple. AI is a power feature, not the main product pitch.
