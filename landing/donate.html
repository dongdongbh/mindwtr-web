Donate

Keep Mindwtr free and independent.

Mindwtr has no ads, no tracking, and no paywalls — and it never will. It's built by one person and kept going by the people who find it useful. If a small contribution feels right, thank you.

Two easy ways to give.

One-off or recurring — whatever suits you. Nothing is gated behind it; a donation just funds the work.

Ko-fi

Quick one-time support — no account or GitHub sign-in needed. Pay by card or PayPal in a few taps.

GitHub Sponsors

Recurring or one-time support, right from GitHub. Best if you already have a GitHub account.

Where the money goes

Exactly what it pays for.

Mindwtr is free and always will be. Donations cover the real costs of building it — the developer tools and services that go into it (the largest ongoing expense), the annual Apple Developer fee that keeps the iOS app on the App Store, and domain renewal — and let me spend more time fixing bugs and building new features.

No pressure

Free either way.

Mindwtr stays free whether or not you donate. If now isn't the time, sharing it, leaving a store review, or reporting bugs helps just as much.
