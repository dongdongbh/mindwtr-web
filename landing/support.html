Support Mindwtr

Help keep Mindwtr free.

Mindwtr is free, open-source, and has no subscription — and it will stay that way. Here are honest ways to keep it going.

Need help?

Have a problem or found a bug?

The best place to report a bug or ask for help is a GitHub issue — it keeps logs, device details, and follow-up together in one trackable place, so nothing gets lost. Please include your platform and app version (Settings → About) so it's easier to help.

  • Open a GitHub issue

    Best for bugs and feature requests — public, searchable, and trackable, so nothing slips.

    Open an issue →

  • Send in-app feedback

    Already in the app? Use Send feedback on the About page (Settings → About) — no GitHub account needed.

  • Email support

    For anything that doesn't fit a public issue, such as a private or account question.

    support@mindwtr.app

Where the money goes

Free forever — here's what it actually costs.

Mindwtr is free and always will be. The real costs are the developer tools and services that go into building it — the largest ongoing expense — plus the annual Apple Developer fee that keeps the iOS app on the App Store (currently paid out of pocket) and domain renewal. Sponsorship covers these and lets me spend more time fixing bugs and building new features.
