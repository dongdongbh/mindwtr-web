GTD-native workflow

A trusted system, not another noisy task list.

Mindwtr follows the shape of Getting Things Done, so capture, review, and focus stay connected instead of becoming separate chores.

  1. 01Capture

    Get it out of your head, fast.

  2. 02Clarify

    Turn vague items into clear next actions.

  3. 03Organize

    Keep projects, contexts, and waiting items where they belong.

  4. 04Reflect

    Review the system before it gets stale.

  5. 05Engage

    Choose the next action with confidence.

Everything in one place

A complete system, not a pile of features.

Every view is built for the GTD method, with depth that stays out of the way until you reach for it.

  • Focus

    A time-based agenda and your next actions, together in one view.

  • Board

    Kanban-style drag and drop across Inbox, Next, Waiting, and Someday.

  • Calendar

    Plan dated work and subscribe to external calendars and ICS feeds.

  • Contexts & tags

    Slash-delimited contexts like @work/meetings with parent matching.

  • Global search

    Operators such as status:, context:, and due:<=7d across everything.

  • Audio capture

    Speak a task; it's transcribed and filed into your inbox automatically.

  • Pomodoro Focus

    15/3, 25/5, and 50/10 timers, plus one custom preset, inside Focus.

  • Reusable lists

    Duplicate tasks or reset checklists for routines you run again and again.

  • Fluid recurrence

    The next date is calculated after you finish, not on a fixed schedule.
